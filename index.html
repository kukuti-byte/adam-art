About the Artist

Salihu Adamu (ADAM Art ) is a Nigeria Visual Artist from Abuja whose works inspiration draw deeply from Culture, Identity, Nature, Socialization Are the expressive power of forms. He studied Fine And Applied Arts and Graduate as the best student in the department At F.C.T College of Education Zuba Abuja, Where he developed a motivation and strong foundation in Painting, Drawing, Graphic and mixed media Techniques. His works are known for it's vibrant bold colours, symbolic element, and emotionally charged that highlight the beauty of Every Nigeria and Africa Stories at large. His creative journey Began since he was 10 but fully accept the call in 2020 and his works has taken him to some notable places Including the formal F.c.t Senate Tanimu Aduda, MD FAAN Kabir Yusuf Mohamed, Formal Commissioner PCC Abuja Musa Ezekiel and Has Exhibition at the F.c.t College of Education Zuba where he showcase his Masterpiece Expanding him reach within the growing community of young Artist and African creatives making an impact
